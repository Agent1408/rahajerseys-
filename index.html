Get the Right Fit

SIZE GUIDE

Use your chest measurement to find your perfect jersey size. Sizes run true to standard football fit.

Size Chest (cm) Chest (inches) Length (cm) Best For
XS84 – 8833 – 35"68Slim/petite build, youth adults
S88 – 9435 – 37"71Slim to average build
M94 – 10037 – 39"74Average build — most popular size
L100 – 10639 – 42"76Broader chest, relaxed fit on medium build
XL106 – 11442 – 45"79Larger build or prefer a loose fit
XXL114 – 12245 – 48"82Big & tall, very relaxed fit
📏 How to measure your chest: Wrap a tape measure around the fullest part of your chest, just under your arms. Keep it level and add 2–4 cm for a comfortable jersey fit.

🏋️ Athletic Build

If you have broad shoulders or a muscular chest, size up one — e.g. if your chest says M, go for L for a comfortable fit through the shoulders.

👕 Slim Fit vs Relaxed

Our jerseys have a standard football fit. For a body-hugging look, stay true to your measurement. For a looser, casual wear feel, size up one.

👦 Buying for a Child?

We recommend XS or S for teenagers. For younger kids, message us on WhatsApp and we'll help you find the right fit based on their age and height.

❓ Not Sure?

Just WhatsApp us your height, weight or chest size and we'll recommend the right size. Getting it right first time saves everyone time! 😊
